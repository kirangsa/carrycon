Apart from DMRC we have completed several Telecom Projects for reputed clients like Mahanagar Telephone Nigam Limited, Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited, Telecommunication Consultant India Ltd., Bharti Airtel Limited, Vodafone & Reliance Infocom Ltd.

Horizontal directional drilling machine (HHD) is used for installation of cables as well as pipes of steels and Plastic under streets, rivers, drains, highways, railways without any digging and disturbance to traffic and Pedestrians. It has tremendous edge over conventional method. of installation of cables & pipes by digging, This “ NO DIG” method or trenchless technology helps to avoid cutting of roads, Highways and streets adding convenience to the passing vehicular and pedestrian traffic as well as ruling out possibilities of accidents. It not only saving in terms of cost and also avoid lot of inconvenience to the public at large while constructing the vital utilities for Telecom, Electric, Water and Sewerage.

Water Management

Sewer and Drainage Rehabilitation

The integrity of pipework systems is constantly challenged, pipelines may crack and joints may fail as a result of; ground loading, subsidence, root infiltration, corrosion, erosion or faulty installation. Excavation to replace failed drains, sewers and chambers invariably involves deep excavations and lengthy project completion times resulting in substantial disruption to the system, its users and the local community. The company is engaged in the support facilitation for this sewerage and drainage rehabilitation by extending service of cleaning the existing sewerage network
